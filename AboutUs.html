About Us

Hello, I'm Halil. Semiksco is a natural soap enterprise established in the Netherlands in 2022. "Of course natural", we started this way with the slogan and now we serve millions. Everyone deserves naturalness.

About my Soap

Handmade soap is not only therapeutic to make, but also a great way to infuse the healing properties of plants and natural clays into something that touches your skin every day. My signature soap recipes, contain pure and powerful ingredients. These soaps incorporate only the best ingredients sourced from sustainable and local suppliers.These soaps contain olive oil, sweet almond oil, organic coconut oil, castor oil and raw organic shea butter in different proportions.My soaps contain organic essential oils, freshly dried herbs & botanicals, natural colorants, exfoliants and natural clays.My soaps have the necessary safety reports and fully comply with the 1223/2009 European cosmetic regulations, made under GMP and registered with the CPNP.
